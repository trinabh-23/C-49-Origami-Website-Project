Interesting Facts About Origami

  1. The word ‘Origami’ comes from two words in Japanese, ‘Ori’, which means folded and ‘kami’, meaning paper.

  2. It is unsure whether Origami originated in Japan or China.

  3. Folding materials, such as leather, damp leaves, and cloth, before paper was invented, has been found on ancient records and Origami practices have passed down through generations.

  4. Origami today is enjoyed by many people globally. There are classes and competitions hosted all over the planet.
